Regulations

Election Rules & Conduct

All candidates, voters, and supporters must comply with the commission's official regulations.

🎓

Eligibility to Run

Candidates must be currently enrolled PSU Pattani students with a minimum GPA of 2.00, with no pending disciplinary action from the university.

🗳️

Voter Eligibility

All full-time students registered at PSU Pattani Campus are eligible to vote. Bring a valid student ID to any polling station on election day.

📢

Campaign Conduct

Campaigning is only permitted during the official campaign period. No posters, events, or online promotion is allowed outside designated zones and timeframes.

🚫

Prohibited Activities

Vote-buying, threats, intimidation, or spreading misinformation are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification and disciplinary referral.

💰

Campaign Spending Limit

Each candidate may not spend more than ฿3,000 on campaign materials. All expenses must be declared to the commission within 7 days of election day.

⚖️

Complaints & Appeals

Any student may file a formal complaint with the commission within 48 hours of an alleged violation. All rulings are final after the appeal review period.
